Bishop

Bishop is a mutant tracker turned time traveler from the year 2055 where, due to an assassination in the 90s, the world is now ruled by Sentinels and all of the X-Men are dead, except Wolverine. Bishop is then sent to the past to stop the event that caused his future to be that way and he meets the X-Men.

Later, in season 4, he gets trapped in the 90's due to his time traveling equipment being broken, so he joins the X-Men while he's unable to go back to his time.
