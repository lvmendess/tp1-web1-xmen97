Magneto

Erik Magnus Lehnsherr is a holocaust survivor. Having witnessed his parents' death by the hand of the Nazi regime and, later, being hunted himself because of his mutant abilities, Erik concluded that humanity would never accept mutants in their own society because, according to him, mutants are the next step in human evolution and, as such, biologically superior to them.

He worked alongside Charles Xavier in a psychiatric hospital after the war and the two formed a friendship. But the contrast between their opinions on the matter of mutant-human coexistence was always on the way and when Charles founded the X-Men, the two drifted apart.
