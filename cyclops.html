Cyclops

Scott Summers' parents supposedly died in a plane crash when was a child, leaving him to grow up in an orphanage. When his mutant abilities manifested, his colleagues started to harass and bully him, until one Charles Xavier came to the orphanage and took him to his School For The Gifted.

There, he became an essential part of the first class of X-Men, alongside other teammates and his future wife, Jean Grey.
