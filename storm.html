Due to the death of her parents in a collapsing building - which gave he a lifelong claustrophobia - Ororo Munroe spent her childhood as an orphan in Cairo, Egypt working as a thief for the Shadow King, an entity disguised as a local crime lord. One time, Ororo pick pocketed a traveling American telepath, Charles Xavier. Xavier found the Shadow King and battled him telepathically, managing to banish him to another plane of existence, freeing Ororo and the other orphans of Cairo.
