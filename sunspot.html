Roberto da Costa

The heir to the fortune of Da Costa family, Roberto was afraid to tell his parents that he was a mutant. He was kidnapped by the Friends of Humanity but rescued by the X-Men, who took him to the X-Mansion.

Later in the show ('97), he and Jubilee are chased after by Bastion's sentinels until he reaches his parents house, where they're having a party. Afraid of receiving backlash, his mom tells him to surrender to the sentinels. He's able to escape with Jubilee and joins the X-Men. Altough, he sides with Magneto at the end, still heartbroken that his mom chose to give him away to sentinels instead of protecting him.

  • Powers: Solar radiation absorption, flight, super strength, shooting spheres of plasma
  • Nationality: Brazilian
  • *He doesn't appear in the X-Men: The Animated Series*
