"X-Men '97"(2024) is a direct sequel to the original 90's tv show "X-Men: The Animated Series".
The story picks up about 9 months after Charles Xavier's (a.k.a Professor X) death in the 90's show
(X-Men:TAS S6E10 "Graduation Day") and follows the mutant team as they face new challenges
in various aspects of their lives, such as leadership, parenthood, trust, love, betrayal, hope,
loss and death, as well as living in an intolerant world that continues to descriminate them,
despite their efforts to prove that mutants aren't a threat to non-mutant humans.
