"X-Men '97"(2024) is a direct sequel to the original 90's tv show "X-Men: The Animated Series"(1992-1996),
both of which take place in Earth-92131. The story picks up about 9 months after Charles Xavier's (a.k.a. Professor X)
death in the 90's show (X-Men:TAS S5E10 "Graduation Day") and follows the mutant team as
they face new challenges in various aspects of their lives, such as leadership, parenthood,
trust, love, betrayal, loss and death, as well as living in an intolerant world that continues to
descriminate them, despite their efforts to prove that mutants aren't a threat to non-mutant humans.
