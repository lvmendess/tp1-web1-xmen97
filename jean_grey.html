Jean Grey

Jean's telepathic powers first manifested as a child, when she witnessed the death of her best friend Annie Richardson. Due to their similar telekinesis abilities, Jean Grey was found by Charles Xavier who offered her parents to help control her powers. At Xavier's Westchester mansion, he helped her learn to control her mind-reading powers, as well as train her to use her mind to manipulate objects, becoming the first X-Men.

  • Powers: Telekinesis, telepathy flight (by using her mind to levitate her body)
