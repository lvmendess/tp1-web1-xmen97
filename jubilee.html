Jubilee

A 13 year old Jubilee was found by the X-Men Storm, Rogue and Gambit when a Sentinel was sent to capture her at a shopping mall and she was rescued by the X-Men and taken to their headquarters (Xavier's Westchester mansion) so they could protect her, should another Sentinel try to capture her again.

However, she escaped and tried to return to her adoptive parents' home when 2 other Sentinels appeared, doped her with a gas that knocked her unconscious and took her to one of their manufacturing facilities. There, the X-Men rescued her once again and she decided to leave her parents' home and join them.
